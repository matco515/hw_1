About Me

Hello, My name is John but I go by Mateo outside of class. I am originally from Vermont but was living in LA prior to relocating to Chicago. Although I miss the warm Los Angeles weather I’ve found I’m able to keep up with my hobbies here. Some of my hobbies are djing, making music, photography, and playing soccer. I tend to try and keep a busy social schedule and really enjoy getting out and meeting new people.

My interest in technology and my drive to develop as a UX/UI designer is what made me come to this bootcamp. I look forward to applying to UX/UI positions that have a front end component when I’m done with the program. I hope everything I learn here will allow me to advance my career.
